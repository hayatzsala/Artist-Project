Sezen Aksu

is a Turkish pop music singer, songwriter and producer

Sezen Aksu born on 13 July 1954,is a Turkish pop music singer, songwriter and producer who has sold over 40 million albums worldwide. Her nicknames include the "Queen of Turkish Pop" and "Minik Serçe"("Little Sparrow").
